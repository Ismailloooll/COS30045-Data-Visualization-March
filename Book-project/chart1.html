Enforcement Trends Over Time

Track how road safety enforcement has changed across Australia from 2008 to 2023. Filter by state and offence type, or switch to an indexed view to compare rates of change.

📊 Key Insight

Mobile phone infringements rose by over 2,000% between 2008 and 2023, reflecting both increased enforcement and near-universal smartphone adoption. Drug driving detections surged even more steeply — up nearly 3,760% — driven by expanded roadside drug testing programs. Drink driving declined steadily while speeding remained the highest-volume offence throughout.
