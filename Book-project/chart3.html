Age Group Breakdown 2023 New Data

Age group breakdowns were first collected in 2023. Explore how road safety offences are distributed across different age cohorts, and which groups are most overrepresented in each category.

🆕 New in 2023 Dataset

Age group data was not available before 2023. This new dimension reveals that the 26–39 age group commits the most offences in absolute terms across all categories. However, the 18–25 group shows the highest rate of drug-driving and mobile phone use relative to their population share, suggesting targeted enforcement may be warranted for younger drivers.
