📊 Key Insight

New South Wales and Victoria consistently issue the most infringements in absolute terms, reflecting their larger populations. However on a per-capita basis, smaller jurisdictions like the Northern Territory show higher rates for drink and drug driving. Victoria is the national leader for mobile phone enforcement in absolute terms from 2016 onward.
