Location Type Analysis 2023 New Data

Location breakdowns are new in 2023. Compare how enforcement differs across metropolitan, regional, rural and remote areas for each offence type.

🆕 New in 2023 Dataset

Metropolitan areas account for the largest share of infringements by volume across all offence types — consistent with population density. However, the composition differs outside cities: drink driving and drug driving make up a higher proportion of total infringements in rural and remote areas, suggesting different enforcement priorities and driver behaviour patterns away from urban centres.
