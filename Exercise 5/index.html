01 · Scatter Plot

Energy vs Star Rating

Each dot is one TV model. Colour shows screen technology. Higher star ratings generally mean lower energy use — hover any dot to see full details.

💡 Higher star ratings correlate with lower energy consumption, but OLED models consistently use more energy than LCD/LED at the same star rating.
02 · Donut Chart

Energy by Technology — All Sizes

Mean annual energy consumption across all screen sizes. OLED consumes significantly more than LCD and LED variants.

03 · Bar Chart

Energy by Technology — 55" Only

For 55-inch TVs specifically. The technology gap narrows but OLED still leads in energy consumption.

04 · Line Chart

Electricity Spot Prices 1998–2024

Rising electricity prices across Australian states make energy-efficient TVs increasingly important. Hover the chart to compare all state prices for any year.

💡 South Australia shows the highest price volatility. The national average has risen sharply since 2015 — making every kWh saved more valuable for consumers.
