01 · Histogram

Energy Consumption Distribution

Distribution of annual energy consumption (kWh/year) across all TV models. Use the filter buttons to show only LED, LCD, or OLED models and see how the distribution changes.

Click a button to filter — click again to deactivate.

💡 LED TVs dominate the market and show the widest range of energy consumption. OLED models cluster at higher consumption levels while LCD tends to be more moderate.
02 · Scatter Plot

Energy vs Star Rating

Each dot represents one TV model. Colour indicates screen technology. Hover any dot to see the brand, model, screen size, and energy consumption details.

💡 Higher star ratings generally correspond to lower energy use. OLED models tend to consume more energy even at higher star ratings compared to LED equivalents.
