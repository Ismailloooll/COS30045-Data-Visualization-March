Key Findings

What does the data tell us?

Three key findings from 15 years of Australian road safety enforcement data.

01
📱

Mobile Phone Fines Have Surged

Mobile phone infringements rose over 2,000% between 2008 and 2023. Drug driving detections rose even more steeply — nearly 3,760%.

See trend chart
02
👤

18–39 Year Olds Dominate Offences

The 2023 age-group data shows drivers aged 18–39 account for over 60% of drug-driving and mobile phone offences nationally.

See age breakdown
03
📍

Metro Areas Lead Enforcement

Metropolitan areas account for the majority of all infringement types — but rural and remote areas show proportionally higher drink and drug driving rates.
