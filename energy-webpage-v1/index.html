Understanding Energy Around the World

This project explores global energy sources, consumption trends, and the shift toward renewable energy.

Energy sources

Solar power

Sunlight is converted into electricity using photovoltaic panels. Solar is one of the fastest-growing energy sources globally.

Wind power

Wind turbines harness kinetic energy from the wind, installed on land and offshore around the world.

Hydropower

Water flowing through dams generates electricity. It is one of the oldest forms of renewable energy.

About this project

This website was created as part of the COS30045 Data Visualization unit at Swinburne University. The goal is to present energy data in a clear and accessible way using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Future versions will include interactive charts and maps built with D3.js.
